Rep. Jim Cooper

Representative for Tennessee’s 5th District

Cooper is the representative for Tennessee’s 5th congressional district and is a Democrat. He has served since Jan 7, 2003. Cooper is next up for reelection in 2020. He was previously the representative for Tennessee’s 4th congressional district as a Democrat from 1983 to 1994.
